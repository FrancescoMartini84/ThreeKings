King Solomon

Had twelve officers over all Israel, which provide victuals for the king and his household: each man his month in a year made provision.

Darius The Great

Set over the kingdom an hundred and twenty princes, which should be over the whole kingdom. Over these set he three presidents, so that the princes might give accounts unto them, and the king should have no damage.

Jesus

Went about all the cities healing every sickness and every disease among the people. When he'd called unto him his twelve disciples, he empowered them to heal all manner of sickness and all manner of disease.
